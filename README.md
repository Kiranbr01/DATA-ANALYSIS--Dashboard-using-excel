# **Sales Data Analysis Project**


## **1. Project Overview**
The **Sales Data Analysis Project** is developed to automate, analyze, and visualize sales performance data using Microsoft Excel.  
The repository contains a macro-enabled workbook named **`salesdata2.xlsm`**, designed to simplify data analysis through automation, pivot-based summaries, and dashboard visualization.

This project highlights the application of Excel’s advanced functionalities—such as VBA macros, pivot tables, and charts—in transforming raw data into actionable business insights

## **2. Project Objectives**
The primary objectives of this project are:

1. To automate sales data entry, cleaning, and analysis using Excel macros (VBA).  
2. To summarize sales data by product, region, and time period.  
3. To identify trends and performance variations through dashboards and charts.  
4. To measure and evaluate key performance indicators (KPIs) for business growth.  
5. To demonstrate Excel as an effective analytical tool for data-driven decision-making.

## **3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

| **No.** | **KPI Name** | **Description** |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Total Sales Revenue | Total income generated from sales over a specific period. |
| 2 | Average Sales per Product | Mean revenue earned per product across all transactions. |
| 3 | Total Units Sold | Sum of all units sold during the reporting period. |
| 4 | Sales Growth Rate | Percentage change in sales compared to the previous period. |
| 5 | Regional Sales Contribution | Percentage share of each region in total sales. |
| 6 | Top Performing Product | The product generating the highest sales. |
| 7 | Customer Retention Rate | Percentage of repeat customers over total customers. |
| 8 | Profit Margin | Ratio of profit to total revenue. |
| 9 | Monthly Sales Trend | Variation of sales performance across months. |
| 10 | Sales Target Achievement | Percentage of actual sales achieved against target goals. |
